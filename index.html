Shallow Valley Caving Club

Learn the art of spelunking

Come join our adventerous group of cave explorers and sign up for our next expedition!

Enjoy rock climbing, hiking, diving or canyoning? Then caving could provide you a new thrilling sense of adventure. Beginners welcome! We provide professional caving techniques, basic equipment and safety training, and hands-on application exploring our local cave systems. This is all in preparation for our annual caving expedition event where everything culminates into a 2-week long exploration of a natural subterranean system chosen by our members.

Shallow Valley Caving Club is a member and community sponsored organization.
